## Requirements

### Requirement: Bounty.ninja Branding

User-facing metadata and navigation SHALL use **Bounty.ninja**, "bounty", and
"bounties", never the former task-board brand or task terminology. The canonical
URL SHALL be `https://bounty.ninja`; app name, URL, description, links, and asset
paths SHALL come from `src/lib/config.ts` where runtime configuration is needed.

### Requirement: Social And Document Metadata

The application SHALL provide a responsive viewport, UTF-8 charset, canonical
URL, description, Open Graph and Twitter large-image metadata, theme color,
favicon, and title identifying Bounty.ninja as a decentralized bounty board.
Metadata SHALL not claim automatic settlement or enabled production payments.

### Requirement: Brand Assets And Accessibility

The hooded-figure logo and favicon SHALL remain legible in light and dark
themes. The 1200x630 Open Graph image SHALL be optimized for web delivery. Text
zoom SHALL not be disabled and meaningful images SHALL have accessible text.
